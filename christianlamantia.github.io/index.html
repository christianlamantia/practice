My Favourite Destinations

Out of all of the cities I had the chance to visit, I though that Munich was by far the most interesting. Especially because we visited during Oktoberfest, the city had an energy completely unmatched to anywhere else in Europe I traveled.

Naples was also incredibly interesting to visit. I felt a special connection to Naples because of my family's Italian-American heritage and I was able to see the many of our culinary and religious traditions unique to southern italian culture practiced without American influence. I visited Naples as part of my first solo trip and even gained enough confidence to order at restaurants in basic Italian. Perhaps most interesting was the opportunity to meet a long-lost cousin in my grandparents' village a few hours from Naples in the province of Basilicata.
